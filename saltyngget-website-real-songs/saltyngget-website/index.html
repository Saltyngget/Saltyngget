About

The sound of Saltyngget

Saltyngget is the artist persona of Georgi Bajov, shaped for a modern audience that values atmosphere, individuality, and memorable visual identity.

The project blends electronic textures, pop instincts, alternative edge, and cinematic mood into a distinctive world: raw enough to feel personal, polished enough to feel iconic.
